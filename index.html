About me

I'm Navneet Singh, an Electrical Design Engineer with a passion for innovation. I work with FPGA's, CPLDs, and SOCs to engineer solutions that push the boundaries of what's achievable. My expertise shines in hardware and firmware design, where I excel at breathing life into embedded electronics boards. In my free time, you'll often find me immersed in PCB designs and layout, carefully bringing designs to life without releasing the "magic smoke."
